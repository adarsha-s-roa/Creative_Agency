Main campaign (Lead Gen)

Retargeting warm audience
₹1,500 — retarget people who viewed your profile or engaged with your posts

₹500–1,000 — test a second ad variant (different headline) in week 3

📊 KPIs to track weekly

CTR (click-through rate)
Target: 0.4%+ for sponsored content. Below 0.3% → rewrite ad copy.
Lead form completion
Target: 10%+ completion rate. Below 8% → simplify form or change offer.
Cost per lead (CPL)
Target: ₹800–2,500 for mid-funnel. Above ₹3,000 → pause and rethink targeting.
Lead to call rate
Target: 30%+ of leads book a call. Below 20% → improve follow-up speed (call within 2hrs).
Cost per booked call
This is your real metric. CPL ÷ lead-to-call rate = cost per call. Target under ₹5,000.
ROI check
1 client at ₹20K/month = ₹3,000–4,000 your cut. Break even with just 2 clients from ads.

⚡ Matt Gray's exact profile checklist (do this before posting anything)

Your profile is your storefront that never closes. Every post you make sends people here. Optimize this first.

  • Banner image — Show what you do. Text on banner: "Helping Indian brands get leads from LinkedIn, Meta & Google." Use Canva, free template.
  • Profile photo — Real face, good lighting, smile, professional background. No logos.
  • Headline formula — [What you do] + [Who you help] + [Outcome]. Example: "Digital Marketing Consultant | Helping Indian SMEs get 10× more leads from LinkedIn & Meta"
  • About section — First line must hook. "I help Bangalore businesses get consistent leads from digital marketing — without wasting budget." Then story + proof + CTA.
  • Featured section — Pin 2 items: (1) Your best post ever. (2) Link to free audit booking page or Calendly. This converts profile visitors to leads.
  • Experience section — Add current role with results: "Generated X leads for Y clients. Commission-based digital marketing consultant."
  • Creator mode ON — Go to Settings → Visibility → Creator mode. Unlocks newsletter, follow button, analytics.
  • Contact info — Add email + website/Calendly link so people can reach you directly.
  • Custom URL — Change to linkedin.com/in/yourname (no random numbers). Looks professional.
  • Skills section — Add: Digital Marketing, LinkedIn Marketing, Meta Ads, Google Ads, Lead Generation, Social Media Marketing, Content Strategy.
  • Follow 20 creators — Follow top digital marketing voices + Bangalore business owners in your niche. Their posts show in your feed = content ideas.
  • Start LinkedIn Newsletter — Create a newsletter (e.g. "Marketing That Works for Indian Businesses"). Subscribers get email notification every time you publish.

📋 5-step group system (do this weekly)

Week 1: Observe only
Join 8–10 groups. Don't post. Read what people struggle with. Note their exact language — use this in your posts and DMs.
Week 2: Answer questions
When someone posts "which platform should I use?" — answer with 3–4 helpful lines. No pitching. Sign off with name and role only.
Week 3: Post value content
Share your best-performing LinkedIn posts into groups. Already-proven content. Groups amplify what's already worked on your feed.
Week 3–4: Warm DMs
After commenting on someone's post or they liked yours: "Hi [Name], I saw your question in [Group] about [topic] — I've helped a few businesses with exactly this. Happy to share?"
Month 2: Create own group
Create "Digital Marketing for Indian Businesses." Invite connections. Post weekly insights. Being the admin = permanent authority position.
Secret advantage
LinkedIn lets you message group members WITHOUT being connected. Bypasses the weekly connection request limit entirely.

⏰ Timing

Post Tuesday–Thursday 8–10 AM IST. This is when Indian decision-makers check LinkedIn before meetings. Avoid Friday afternoon and all weekend. The first 90 minutes after posting determine your total reach — stay online and reply to every comment.

💬 Comments beat likes 7×

Comments carry 7× more algorithmic weight than likes. End every post with ONE specific question — not "What do you think?" but "Which of these 5 mistakes is costing Indian businesses the most in your experience?"

🔗 Never link in post body

LinkedIn penalizes external links in posts by 3×. Always put links in the first comment after publishing. Write "Link in first comment 👇" in the post body. This single change can double your organic reach instantly.

📌 Saves matter more than likes

In 2026, saves are weighted higher than likes in the algorithm. Create "save this for later" content — checklists, step-by-step frameworks, comparison guides. Carousels get the most saves of any format.

🎥 Vertical video = biggest reach

Short-form vertical video (15–60 sec, portrait) gets 5–10× more reach via LinkedIn's new video feed. Always add captions — 85% watch without sound. Talk about one real result, one real mistake, or one hot take.

👤 Personal > Company page

LinkedIn's algorithm gives personal profiles 561% more reach than company pages. Your personal page does the heavy lifting. The company page is for credibility when prospects check you out. Post content from YOUR profile first.

🔄 Repurpose winners

High-performing text post → carousel. High-save carousel → video. High-engagement video → newsletter article. One great idea = 4 pieces of content = 4× reach. Never let a winning idea die after one format.

👥 The 25-person rule (Matt Gray)

Matt Gray ensures 25 people engage with his content in the first 45 minutes. Build a group of 10–15 colleagues, fellow marketers, or clients who agree to genuinely comment on each other's posts immediately after publishing. Authentic, real comments only.

📊 Review monthly

Every 30 days review your top 3 performing posts. What format? What topic? What hook style? Double down on what worked. Kill what didn't. Matt Gray reviews all high-performing content on the platform and reformulates strategy monthly.
